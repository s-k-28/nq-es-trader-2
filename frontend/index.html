Trading Models

OU Reversion

Priority: 15   Risk: $2,500
Ornstein-Uhlenbeck mean-reversion on price-VWAP deviation. Trades when half-life is short and Hurst < 0.45. Quality-filtered at Q >= 4.

PD Level Reversion

Priority: 22   Risk: $1,200
Fades at previous day high/low with reversal candle confirmation. Institutional reference levels where large orders cluster.

VWAP Reversion

Priority: 25   Risk: $600
Bidirectional VWAP z-score fade. Short when z > 2.0, long when z < -2.0. Targets snap-back to session VWAP.

Opening Range Reversion

Priority: 28   Risk: $600
Fades extended moves beyond the 15-min opening range back to OR midpoint. Window: 10:00 - 12:00 ET.

EMA Reversion

Priority: 30   Risk: $600
Fades when price extends 2.5+ standard deviations from the 20-period EMA. Window: 9:50 - 14:30 ET.

Sweep Reversal

Priority: 35   Risk: $600
Detects liquidity sweeps at PDH/PDL and session extremes. Fades the stop hunt with immediate reversal confirmation.

Kalman Momentum

Priority: 40   Risk: $600
Trades Kalman filter slope direction when Hurst >= 0.5. Requires 5-bar slope consistency. Window: 10:15 - 14:00 ET.

Trend Continuation

Priority: 40   Risk: $600
Follows EMA/regime trends with fair value gap pullback entries. Requires EMA alignment over 10 bars.

PM Momentum

Priority: 50   Risk: $600
Afternoon session Kalman slope pullback model. Window: 13:30 - 15:00 ET.
